All services are delivered by CISRS-certified scaffolders based in Rayleigh. Call 01702 820468 for a free quote.

Residential scaffolding is designed for domestic properties — houses, bungalows, flats and extensions. The priority is working considerately around occupied homes, neighbours and tight residential roads. Access, aesthetics and speed of erection all matter.

Commercial scaffolding involves larger structures, more complex designs, longer hire periods and formal documentation — including method statements, risk assessments and sometimes highway licences. Commercial projects often require bespoke scaffold configurations to meet site-specific needs.

Axis Scaffolding Essex handles both — from a single chimney in Rayleigh to a full commercial development in Chelmsford or Basildon.

Roof scaffolding provides safe working access to all or part of a roof. It typically includes a full scaffold frame around the property with boarded working platforms at eaves and ridge level, allowing roofers, chimney engineers and guttering specialists to work safely.

You will almost certainly need roof scaffolding for:

  • Full roof replacements or re-tiles
  • Chimney repointing, removal or rebuilding
  • Roofline replacement (fascias, soffits, gutters) on two-storey properties
  • Any roof work where a roofer needs to walk on or close to the roofline

Even for smaller repairs, UK health and safety law requires safe working at height. Call 01702 820468 and we'll advise you honestly whether scaffolding is needed for your specific job.
